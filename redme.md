# 🎓 Student Management System

A comprehensive web-based Student Management System built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This system provides a complete solution for managing students, teachers, and administrative tasks in educational institutions.

![CSS3](https://img.shields.io/badge/CSS3-1572B6?style=for-the-badge\&logo=css3\&logoColor=white)
![JavaScript](https://img.shields.io/badge/JavaScript-F7DF1E?style=for-the-badge\&logo=javascript\&logoColor=black)

## 🌟 Features

### 🔐 Multi-Role Authentication System

* **Admin Dashboard** - Complete system management
* **Teacher Dashboard** - Class and student management
* **Student Dashboard** - Personal academic tracking

### 👨‍🎓 Student Management

* ✅ Student registration with comprehensive form validation
* ✅ Profile management with photo upload
* ✅ Personal information editing
* ✅ Academic progress tracking

### 📊 Attendance Management

* ✅ Daily attendance marking (Teacher/Admin)
* ✅ Subject-wise attendance tracking
* ✅ Attendance reports and statistics
* ✅ Student attendance history

### 📈 Marks & Results Management

* ✅ Marks entry for different exam types
* ✅ Automatic percentage and grade calculation
* ✅ Performance analytics
* ✅ Individual student progress reports

### 📚 Subject Management

* ✅ Subject creation and management
* ✅ Class-wise subject allocation
* ✅ Teacher assignment to subjects
* ✅ Subject details and descriptions

### 📞 Contact & Support

* ✅ Contact form with validation
* ✅ Department contact information
* ✅ FAQ section
* ✅ Social media integration
